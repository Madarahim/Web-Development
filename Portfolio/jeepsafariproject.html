Details:

This is a website I created that modeled after a company service that I would love, Jeep Safaris in California. Its provided for 3 different National Parks in California: Yosemite National Park, Joshua Tree National Park, and Los Padres National Forest. I created it using basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It provides 3 different safari packages depending on how many individual tickets or jeeps you want during your own personal Safari. Each package has a PayPal button created that links to and deposits payments to my account. There are also options that link to other websites that provide more information on California's wildlife. It also has basic website pages like terms of service and contact.
