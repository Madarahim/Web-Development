Details:

This is a project that I am developing in order to have a place where Pet Fosters can network and stay connected on a social-media site. This is being developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. The home page is inspired by and modeled after the Facebook home page layout. I usually run into stray dogs in my neighborhood, and I hated taking them to the shelter because theres a chance they could be euthanized. I'm fortunate that all the dogs I rescued have been adopted, but I would love to easily connect with a potential foster in my area, so that I may take whatever homeless pet I find to them, since I can't take care of them myself. There are hurdles to this project, like doing background checks for people who register as potential fosters, but I loved the idea so I thought I'd give it a shot.
